Chicken Parm

Description

Chicken Parm, short for Chicken Parmesan or Chicken Parmigiana, is a classic Italian-American dish consisting of breaded chicken cutlets, fried, and then topped with tomato sauce and melted mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.

The combination of crispy chicken, tangy sauce, and gooey cheese makes it a comforting and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Sauce
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Prepare the Chicken
  2. Bread the Chicken
  3. Pan-Fry the Chicken
  4. Bake the Chicken Parm
  5. Serve!
