Chili

Description

Chili is a bold, hearty, and spicy stew that’s all about deep, smoky flavors and slow-cooked perfection. It’s a Tex-Mex classic with roots in Mexican and American cowboy cuisine, originally made with dried chilies, meat, and spices.

Chili con carne (“chili with meat”) dates back to the 1700s in Texas, influenced by Mexican and Spanish flavors. It became a frontier favorite for cowboys and gold rush travelers because it was easy to make in big batches with preserved ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Chilies & Spices
  • Tomatoes & Broth
  • Beans (optional)
  • Onions & Garlic

Steps

  1. Sear the Meat
  2. Toast the Spices
  3. Add Tomato & Broth
  4. Simmer & Thicken
  5. Finish with Breans
  6. Add Toppings & Eat!
